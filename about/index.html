About

For most of my life, if someone asked me if I was a creative person I would have responded with a comfortable, “No.” To me, creativity was about art or artistic ability, certainly things I never had. In the end, creativity is simpler than that. Creativity is about creation, not art. Engineers are creative, programmers are creative. A novel solution to a problem requires inspiration, insight, and yes, creativity. I love working in an agile programming environment and the freedom to pursue creative solutions to interesting problems. This site chronicles some of my thoughts on computer programming, hardware design, school, and work.
